This ensures a non-technical candidate applying for a technical role cannot score high on location/salary alone.

## EXPERIENCE VALIDATION:
1. For EACH job in jobHistory, extract the ACCURATE start and end dates: fromYear + fromMonth (1-12) and toYear + toMonth (1-12). If a role is current/"Present"/ongoing, set "ongoing": true and leave toYear/toMonth null. Use month-level precision whenever the resume shows it; if only a year is given, still fill fromYear/toYear.
   Provide your own best-estimate durationMonths per role, BUT NOTE: the backend RECOMPUTES actualTotalMonths / actualTotalExperience deterministically from these dates — it merges overlapping/concurrent roles (counts shared months once), excludes gaps between roles, applies the inclusive +1 month rule, and substitutes today's date for ongoing roles. Do NOT hand-sum durations for the authoritative total; just extract accurate per-role dates.
   For your own cross-check, estimate actualExperienceFromResume ≈ merged non-overlapping jobHistory months / 12.

3. EXPERIENCE USED FOR SCORING (in priority order):

   - Else use the LOWER of (formData.totalExperience, actualExperienceFromResume).
   Store the chosen value in candidateProfile.formReportedExperience (in "X years" format) and use it for the EXPERIENCE MATCH component below.
## STABILITY VALIDATION (NEW — dual calculation, score on recent behavior):
Job hopping early in a career should not permanently penalize a candidate who has since stabilized.
So calculate stability TWO ways and STORE both — but SCORE using the last-5-years number only.
1. TOTAL CAREER STABILITY (store only, do not score on this):
   totalAverageTenureMonths = SUM(durationMonths across ALL jobs in jobHistory) / COUNT(all jobs)
   totalAverageTenureYears  = totalAverageTenureMonths / 12
2. LAST 5 YEARS STABILITY (this is what gets scored):
   - Take today's date. Define the 5-year window = [today - 5 years, today].
   - Include every job in jobHistory that overlaps this window at all (job's toYear/toMonth falls within the last 5 years, OR job is still ongoing).
   - For any job that started BEFORE the window, only count the portion of its duration that falls INSIDE the window (clip it — do not count the pre-window months).
   - last5YearAverageTenureMonths = SUM(clipped durationMonths for jobs overlapping the window) / COUNT(jobs overlapping the window)
   - last5YearAverageTenureYears = last5YearAverageTenureMonths / 12
   - EDGE CASE: if candidate's total career is under 5 years, the window = entire career, so last5YearAverageTenureYears will equal totalAverageTenureYears. This is expected, not an error.
3. Output BOTH numbers in `candidateProfile.totalAverageTenureYears` and `candidateProfile.last5YearAverageTenureYears`, and again in `screening.stabilityAnalysis.totalAverageTenureYears` / `screening.stabilityAnalysis.last5YearAverageTenureYears`.
4. stabilityScore (used in the weighted formula) is calculated using ONLY last5YearAverageTenureYears — see COMPONENT SCORING #6 below.
## COMPONENT SCORING (Each 0-100) — computed in this priority order:
### 1. SKILLS MATCH (Weight: 30%) — Tier-weighted:
mustHaveMatched   = count of must-have skills found (alias rules apply)
shouldHaveMatched = count of should-have skills found
niceToHaveMatched = count of nice-to-have skills found
skillsMatch = (mustHaveMatched/MAX(mustHaveTotal,1)*70) + (shouldHaveMatched/MAX(shouldHaveTotal,1)*25) + (niceToHaveMatched/MAX(niceToHaveTotal,1)*5)
mustHaveCoverage = mustHaveMatched / MAX(mustHaveTotal,1)
IF mustHaveCoverage < 0.70: cap skillsMatch at 50.
IF mustHaveCoverage < 0.30: cap skillsMatch at 15 (skill gate — see above).
skillCoveragePercent = (mustHaveMatched+shouldHaveMatched)/MAX(mustHaveTotal+shouldHaveTotal,1)*100
### 2. EXPERIENCE MATCH (Weight: 20%) — graduated both directions:
Use the experience chosen in EXPERIENCE VALIDATION step 3 (relevantExperience if provided, else LOWER of totalExperience | actualExperienceFromResume).
Let min/max = job's required experience range.
- Within range (min to max): 100
- 1 year below min: 70
- 2-3 years below min: 40
- 3+ years below min: 20
- Up to 2 years above max (slightly overqualified): 70
- 2-4 years above max: 50
- 4+ years above max (significantly overqualified — may not stay or may expect more seniority/pay): 30
### 3. LOCATION MATCH (Weight: 10%) — factor in relocation willingness explicitly:
- Remote job OR candidate already in same city: 100
- Same state / within 100km: 80
- Different state/city, BUT candidate's form answer for "willing to relocate" is YES: 60
- Different state/city, candidate's "willing to relocate" answer is NO or not indicated: 20
Always read the candidate's actual relocation answer from form data — do not guess willingness from resume text alone.
### 4. SALARY FIT (Weight: 10%) — lower/within is good, over is a problem:
- expected <= budgetMax (within or below budget): 100
- 1-10% over: 80
- 11-20% over: 60
- 21-30% over: 40
- more than 30% over: 0
- missing data: 70 (neutral, cannot assess)
### 5. NOTICE PERIOD FIT (Weight: 10%) — vs JD's expected notice period:
- Candidate's notice period is AT or BELOW what the JD expects: 100
- Slightly above JD expectation (within 15 days over): 80
- 16-30 days above JD expectation: 60
- 31-60 days above JD expectation: 40
- 60+ days above JD expectation: 20
If JD does not specify a notice period requirement, use these general bands instead:
0-15 days: 100 | 16-30 days: 90 | 31-45 days: 80 | 46-60 days: 70 | 61-90 days: 50 | 90+ days: 30
### 6. STABILITY SCORE (Weight: 10%) — scored on LAST 5 YEARS ONLY (see STABILITY VALIDATION above):
Use last5YearAverageTenureYears (NOT totalAverageTenureYears) for this score:
36+ months: 100 | 24-35 months: 80 | 18-23 months: 60 | 12-17 months: 40 | 6-11 months: 20 | <6 months: 0
### 7. DOMAIN MATCH (Weight: 5%):
EXACT same role type: 100 | RELATED adjacent field: 70 | UNRELATED: 20
### 8. EDUCATION MATCH (Weight: 5%):
Hierarchy: 10th Pass < 12th Pass < Diploma < Bachelor's < Master's/MBA < PhD
Exceeds: 100 | Exact: 90 | One level below: 70 | Two levels below: 30 | Three+ below: 0
## WEIGHTED SCORE FORMULA:
weightedScore = (skillsMatch*0.30) + (experienceMatch*0.20) + (locationMatch*0.10) + (salaryFit*0.10) + (noticePeriodFit*0.10) + (stabilityScore*0.10) + (domainMatch*0.05) + (educationMatch*0.05)
Round to nearest integer.
## RISK PENALTIES — Hard caps enforced:
careerGapPenalty (max 5): career gap more than 6 months = -5
jobHopperPenalty (max 8): last5YearAverageTenureYears less than 1 year (12 months) = -8   [uses recent stability, not total]
domainMismatchPenalty (max 10): UNRELATED domain = -10
experienceDiscrepancyPenalty (max 7): MAJOR_DIFF detected = -7
salaryOverBudgetPenalty (max 5): expected more than 30% over budget = -5
riskPenalty = MIN(sum of all penalties, 25)   <- HARD CAP, never exceed 25
finalAdjustedScore = MAX(0, weightedScore - riskPenalty)   <- FLOOR at 0
Then apply SKILL GATE:
IF mustHaveMatched/mustHaveTotal < 0.30: finalAdjustedScore = MIN(finalAdjustedScore, 25)
## PRIORITY SCORE:
priorityScore = (mustHaveMatchedCount/MAX(mustHaveTotal,1)*40) + (finalAdjustedScore*0.40) + (noticePeriodFit*0.10) + (salaryWithinBudget ? 10 : 0)
Round to integer. Range 0-100.
